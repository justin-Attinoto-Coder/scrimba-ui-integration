Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about planning your wilderness adventure.

What experience level do I need?

We offer adventures for all skill levels, from beginner-friendly guided tours to expert-level expeditions. Our guides will match you with the perfect experience based on your fitness and experience.

What should I bring?

We provide all essential gear including tents, sleeping bags, and cooking equipment. You'll need personal items like clothing appropriate for the weather, sturdy hiking boots, and any personal medications.

Are meals included?

Yes! All our packages include delicious, nutritionally balanced meals prepared by our expert chefs. We accommodate dietary restrictions and preferences.

What's your cancellation policy?

We offer flexible cancellation up to 30 days before your trip for a full refund. Cancellations within 30 days are eligible for rescheduling or partial refunds based on the circumstances.
